# Win Conditions

## Default wins

| Winner | Condition |
| --- | --- |
| **Good** | The Demon is dead |
| **Evil** | Exactly **two** living players remain |

Dead players still win or lose with their team.

## Simultaneous wins

If good and evil would both win at the same moment (e.g. Demon dies leaving two alive),
**good wins**.

## Character specials

| Character | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| **Saint** | If the Saint is **executed**, good loses (evil wins). Night death of the Saint does **not** auto-lose. |
| **Mayor** | If only **three** living players remain and **no execution** occurs that day, the Mayor’s team wins. Storyteller may also redirect a night death off the Mayor onto another player. |
| **Scarlet Woman** | If the Demon dies while **5+** players are alive, Scarlet Woman becomes the Imp — game continues. If **4 or fewer** alive when the Demon dies, good wins. |
| **Imp starpass** | If the Imp kills **themself** at night, a living Minion becomes the Imp — game continues. |

## What does not end the game

- A Minion dying (unless they had become the Imp)
- Final three with no execution (unless Mayor’s condition applies)
- A night death that is not the last Demon without SW conversion

## Checklist after each death

1. Did the Demon die?
   - Scarlet Woman in play and 5+ living → convert SW to Imp; continue.
   - Else → **good wins**.
2. Exactly two living players and a living Demon? → **evil wins**.
3. Saint executed? → **evil wins**.
4. Else continue.
