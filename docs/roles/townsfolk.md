# Townsfolk

All Townsfolk are **good**.

---

## Washerwoman

**You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Townsfolk.**

- **When:** First night only.
- **Info:** Shown two players and one Townsfolk character token; exactly one of the two is that character (or registers as that Townsfolk — e.g. Spy).
- **Storyteller:** Marks TOWNSFOLK and WRONG pair in Grimoire.

---

## Librarian

**You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Outsider.**

- **When:** First night only.
- **If no Outsiders in play:** Learn **0** (no Outsider).
- **If Drunk is the Outsider:** Learn Drunk as the Outsider type, not who they think they are.
- **Info shape:** Same two-player + token format as Washerwoman.

---

## Investigator

**You start knowing that 1 of 2 players is a particular Minion.**

- **When:** First night only.
- **Info:** Two players + one Minion character token; one of them is that Minion (Spy may register).

---

## Chef

**You start knowing how many pairs of evil players are neighbors.**

- **When:** First night only.
- **Count:** Adjacent seats both evil = one pair. Three evils in a row = **two** pairs. Circle wraps around.
- **Recluse/Spy:** May register as evil/good for this count (ST choice).

---

## Empath

**Each night, you learn how many of your living neighbors are evil.**

- **Neighbors:** Closest living player clockwise and counterclockwise (skip dead).
- **Result:** 0, 1, or 2.
- Does not say **which** neighbor is evil.

---

## Fortune Teller

**Each night, choose 2 players: you learn if either is a Demon.**

- **Yes** if at least one chosen is a Demon **or** the **red herring**.
- Red herring: one good player selected at setup; registers as Demon **only** to Fortune Teller.
- Can pick self; can pick dead; can be the red herring.
- Does not say which of the two pinged.

---

## Undertaker

**Each night*, you learn which character died by execution today.**

- Only **execution** deaths (not night kills, not exile).
- If no execution death today: no info / do not wake for info.
- If Drunk executed: show **Drunk**, not the bluff Townsfolk.

---

## Monk

**Each night*, choose a player (not yourself): they are safe from the Demon tonight.**

- Blocks Demon kill and other Demon harmful effects that night.
- Does not block execution or non-Demon kills.
- Demon does not get a re-pick if they hit the protected player.

---

## Ravenkeeper

**If you die at night, you are woken to choose a player: you learn their character.**

- Only if death is **at night** (typically Demon).
- Still works while newly dead (ability text triggers on death).
- Fails if drunk/poisoned when killed.

---

## Virgin

**The 1st time you are nominated, if the nominator is a Townsfolk, they are executed immediately.**

- No vote; that is the day’s execution.
- Outsider/Minion/Demon nominator → ability does nothing; vote proceeds.
- Ability spent after first nomination regardless.

---

## Slayer

**Once per game, during the day, publicly choose a player: if they are the Demon, they die.**

- Public declaration required.
- Wrong guess: nothing happens (no confirmation you are Slayer beyond the attempt).
- Correct: Demon dies immediately → check SW / win conditions.
- Spent even if drunk/poisoned at use (fails and cannot retry).

---

## Soldier

**You cannot die at the hand of the Demon.**

- Night Demon attack on Soldier → no death; Demon does not re-target.
- Also protects from other negative Demon effects where applicable (mainly the kill).
- **Can** die by execution and non-Demon effects.

---

## Mayor

**If only 3 players live & no execution occurs, your team wins. If you die at night, the Storyteller may choose another player to die instead.**

- 3-alive no-exec win is for the Mayor’s **team** (normally good).
- Night bounce is optional ST discretion; group does not learn a bounce happened.
- If bounce target is unkillable (Soldier / Monk protect), **nobody** may die that night.
