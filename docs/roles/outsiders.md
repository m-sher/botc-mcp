# Outsiders

Outsiders are **good**, but their abilities create problems or ambiguity for good.

---

## Butler

**Each night, choose a player (not yourself): tomorrow, you may only vote if they are voting too.**

- Master may be living or dead.
- Butler may abstain even if master votes.
- Butler cannot vote if master does not vote (hand must go down if master drops).
- Does not restrict **nominating**.

---

## Drunk

**You do not know you are the Drunk. You think you are a Townsfolk, but your ability is fake.**

- **Setup:** Storyteller assigns the Drunk to a Townsfolk character token; that seat is the Drunk.
- Player draws/looks like that Townsfolk; wakes and acts on that schedule.
- Storyteller may give false information.
- Librarian detects them as the Drunk Outsider, not as their bluff Townsfolk.
- Undertaker shows Drunk if executed.
- Virgin: Drunk nominating Virgin does **not** die (Drunk is not a Townsfolk character).

---

## Recluse

**You might register as evil / Minion / Demon, even if dead.**

- Actually good Outsider with no active night choice.
- Each time an ability would detect them, Storyteller chooses how they register.
- Can vary per detection in the same night.
- Does not gain evil abilities; does not join evil team.

---

## Saint

**If you die by execution, your team loses.**

- Night death is “safe” for good (no auto-loss).
- Encourages good to avoid executing confirmed Saints — and evil to fake Saint.
