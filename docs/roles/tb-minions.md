# Trouble Brewing — Minions

Minions are **evil**. At 7+ players on the first night they learn who the Demon is
(and usually see fellow Minions when woken as a group).

---

## Poisoner

**Each night, choose a player: they are poisoned tonight and tomorrow day.**

- Old poison ends when new night’s Poisoner acts (standard).
- Poisoned player has no ability; may get false info; not told.
- Once-per-game abilities attempted while poisoned are spent and fail.
- If Poisoner dies, their active poison ends immediately.

**Strong N1 targets:** Empath, Fortune Teller, Washerwoman/Librarian/Investigator/Chef info, Virgin, Slayer.

---

## Spy

**Each night, you see the Grimoire. You might register as good / a Townsfolk / an Outsider.**

- Full Grimoire read: characters, reminders, dead, drunk, red herring, etc.
- Registration chosen by Storyteller per detection (Washerwoman, Virgin, Undertaker, Empath, etc.).
- Still evil for win conditions and true alignment.

---

## Scarlet Woman

**If there are 5 or more players alive when the Demon dies, you become the Demon.**

- Travellers typically do not count toward the 5.
- Become the Demon of the script (Imp in TB) with full powers going forward.
- Game does **not** end on that Demon death.
- If Demon dies with **4 or fewer** alive → good wins; SW does not convert.
- Conversion is secret to the town; SW is informed privately.

---

## Baron

**There are extra Outsiders in play. [+2 Outsiders]**

- **Setup only:** +2 Outsider tokens, −2 Townsfolk tokens.
- No night ability.
- Changes world expectations (Librarian info, outsider counts, bluffs).
