# Minions

Minions are **evil**. On the first night with **7+ players** they learn who the Demon is
(and who the other Minions are).

---

## Poisoner

**Each night, choose a player: they are poisoned tonight and tomorrow day.**

- Previous poison ends when the Poisoner acts again.
- Poisoned player has no ability; may get false info; is not told.
- Once-per-game abilities attempted while poisoned are spent and fail.
- If the Poisoner dies, their active poison ends immediately.

Strong early targets: Empath, Fortune Teller, first-night info roles, Virgin, Slayer.

---

## Spy

**Each night, you see the Grimoire. You might register as good / a Townsfolk / an Outsider.**

- Full Grimoire: characters, reminders, dead, drunk, red herring, etc.
- Registration chosen by Storyteller per detection.
- Still evil for wins and true alignment.

---

## Scarlet Woman

**If there are 5 or more players alive when the Demon dies, you become the Imp.**

- Become the Imp with full powers going forward.
- Game does **not** end on that Demon death.
- If the Demon dies with **4 or fewer** alive → good wins; SW does not convert.
- Conversion is secret to the town; SW is informed privately.

---

## Baron

**There are extra Outsiders in play. [+2 Outsiders]**

- **Setup only:** +2 Outsider tokens, −2 Townsfolk.
- No night ability.
