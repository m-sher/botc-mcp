## Play notes

**Good:** share and cross-check information; expect some false results; use Virgin,
Slayer, Undertaker, Ravenkeeper to confirm; track Outsider count for Baron; avoid
executing Saint.

**Evil:** bluff concrete Townsfolk; coordinate Poisoner with Imp kills; use Spy
Grimoire reads; time Scarlet Woman and Imp starpass when under pressure.
