## Gameplay notes

**Good** should:

- Share information carefully; expect some of it to be false.
- Confirm roles via Virgin, Slayer, Undertaker, Ravenkeeper.
- Watch Outsider count for Baron.
- Avoid executing Saint; use night death preference for Saints.

**Evil** should:

- Bluff as specific Townsfolk (Demon has three safe bluffs).
- Coordinate Poisoner targets with Imp kills.
- Use Spy Grimoire knowledge to back up lies.
- Consider SW timing and Imp starpass when under heat.
