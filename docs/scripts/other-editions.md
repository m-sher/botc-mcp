# Other Editions (Overview)

Full role catalogs are out of initial scope; this page orients the eval server.

## Sects & Violets

- **Difficulty:** Intermediate
- **Themes:** Madness, character changes (Pit-Hag), alignment swaps (Snake Charmer),
  always-false Townsfolk info (Vortox), death-triggered disasters (Klutz, Sweetheart)
- **Demons (examples):** Fang Gu, No Dashii, Vortox
- **Minions (examples):** Witch, Cerenovus, Pit-Hag, Evil Twin

## Bad Moon Rising

- **Difficulty:** Advanced
- **Themes:** Multi-kills, day deaths, resurrection, lethal minions
- **Demons (examples):** Po, Pukka, Shabaloth, Zombuul
- **Minions (examples):** Assassin, Devil’s Advocate, Godfather, Mastermind

## Travellers

- Public character identity; **hidden alignment**
- Can join mid-game or leave early
- Exiled rather than executed
- Usually **do not count** toward the two-player evil win
- Enable player counts above 15 (up to 20)

## Fabled

- Controlled by Storyteller, not drawn from the bag
- Used for balance, teaching, or accessibility (e.g. Spirit of Ivory, Duchess)
- Optional; skip for pure eval simulations unless testing ST tools

## Custom scripts

Any subset/combination of characters can form a script via the official script tool.
Each script needs a generated night order. For this repo, **Trouble Brewing is canonical**.
