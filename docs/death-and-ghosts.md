# Death and Ghost Players

## Principle

**Death is not the end.** Dead players still talk, still share their team’s win or loss,
and keep one ghost vote.

## How players die

| Cause | Timing |
| --- | --- |
| Imp attack | Night (announced at dawn) |
| Execution | Day |
| Ability (Slayer, Virgin bounce, etc.) | Day (or as specified) |

## Effects of death

| Keep | Lose |
| --- | --- |
| Talk any time | Character ability (usually immediately) |
| Team win/loss | Right to **nominate** |
| One **ghost vote** for the rest of the game | Unlimited voting |

### Exceptions

Some abilities fire **on death** (e.g. Ravenkeeper if killed at night). Those still fail
if the player is drunk or poisoned at the critical moment.

## Dead players cannot die again

Imp “killing” a dead player does nothing public (no second death announcement).
Useful for sink kills while bluffing Soldier / Monk.

## Ghost vote

1. On death, the player has one remaining vote for the rest of the game.
2. They cast it on **one** nomination total, then never vote again.
3. They still cannot nominate.

## Public information

On death, the group learns **who** died — not character, alignment, or killer.
